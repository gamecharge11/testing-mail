OurProducts

We are manufacturing from pure cotton that is available in indian markets first we make the cloth under high production cspasity machines. That is we can produce 500+ products per day from a machine. Then we make this cloth for mercerizing, after this mercerized it is dyed to an colour and products printed in various designs.


Plus Point of Mercerizing

When we mercerize the cloths then the cloth will get shiny and that will increase the life of cloth, this cloth can be used in winter, autumn and spring seasons. It changes in asper seasons. After printing the cloth will go for 0% shrinkage process. Then it is softened and dry done by machine for strengthing.
